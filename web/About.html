About




       Rolex, a renowned Swiss watch brand, was founded in 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis in London under the name Wilsdorf & Davis. In 1908, the brand name “Rolex” was registered. The company moved to Geneva, Switzerland, in 1919 due to economic challenges in the UK.

Rolex has been a pioneer in watchmaking, achieving several milestones:

1910: First wristwatch to receive the Swiss Certificate of Chronometric Precision.
1926: Introduced the Oyster, the first waterproof wristwatch.
1945: Launched the Datejust, the first wristwatch with an automatically changing date on the dial.
1953: Released the Submariner, the first divers’ watch waterproof to a depth of 100 meters.
Today, Rolex is known for its precision, durability, and timeless design, continuing to innovate in the world of luxury watches.
